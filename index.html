So what exactly is a codefest
A codefest, is where computer science enthusiasts team up and work on ideas related to a theme. These ideas are transformed into innovative products over the course of the next hours.
Kinds of developments accepted
All kinds of applications are encouraged, from web apps and websites to mobile apps and a lot more.
