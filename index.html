What is a Codefest?

A codefest is a platform that provides the opportunity for aspiring developers to showcase their unique talents to both each other and veterans of the industry. Our codefest is Bangalore's first overnight school hackathon and we plan on making it as fulfilling and rewarding an experience as possible.

Themes

With a multitude of impactful themes, the participants will be tackling current technical problems with innovative improvements and solutions.
